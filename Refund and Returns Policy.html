Returns & Refunds

Hassle-free returns with our customer-friendly policy.

Last Updated: 13 February 2026

Return Eligibility

You may return your purchase if it meets the following conditions:

  • Item is returned within 7 days of delivery
  • Item is in original, unused condition with all tags and packaging intact
  • Original invoice and certificate of authenticity are included
  • Item shows no signs of wear, damage, or alteration

Gold Exchange Special

We offer a special exchange program where you can exchange your old gold jewelry for new designs. Our exchange rates are transparent and based on current market rates with minimal making charges.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Customized, personalized, or engraved jewelry
  • Items that have been resized or altered after purchase
  • Products marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Items damaged due to customer mishandling or wear
  • Gift cards and promotional items

Important Note on Hallmarked Gold

Hallmarked gold items require special inspection before exchange. The hallmark must be intact and verifiable. Any tampering with the hallmark will void the return eligibility.

Return Process

Follow these simple steps to return your item:

1

Initiate Return

Contact our customer support within 7 days of delivery at +91 98765 43210 or email support@saikiranjewelleries.com with your order number and reason for return.

2

Verification

Our team will review your request and provide a Return Authorization Number (RAN) along with return instructions within 24-48 hours.

3

Pack & Ship

Securely pack the item in its original packaging with all accessories, tags, and documents. Use the provided return shipping label or arrange insured shipping.

4

Inspection

Once received, our quality team will inspect the item within 3-5 business days to verify condition and authenticity.

5

Refund/Exchange

Upon successful inspection, your refund will be processed within 7-10 business days, or exchange item will be dispatched.

Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued through the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Card: Refund processed to the same card within 7-10 business days
  • UPI/Net Banking: Refund to the source account within 5-7 business days
  • Cash on Delivery: Bank transfer or store credit (your choice)
  • Wallet Payments: Refund to the respective wallet within 24 hours

Note: Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or defective product.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item:

  • Contact us immediately within 24 hours of delivery
  • Share photos of the damaged/defective item and packaging
  • We will arrange a free pickup and expedited replacement or full refund
  • No restocking fee applies for damaged/defective returns
