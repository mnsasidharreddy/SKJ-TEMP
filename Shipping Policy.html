Shipping Policy

Fast, secure, and insured delivery of your precious jewelry.

Last Updated: 13 February 2026

Order Processing

We understand that you're excited to receive your jewelry. Here's how we process orders:

  • Orders are processed within 1-3 business days after payment confirmation
  • Customized or made-to-order pieces may require additional processing time (7-14 days)
  • You will receive an order confirmation email immediately after placing your order
  • A shipping confirmation email with tracking details will be sent once your order is dispatched

Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

Standard Delivery

3-7 business days within major cities. Free shipping on orders above ₹10,000.

Express Delivery

1-2 business days (available in select cities). Additional charges apply based on location.

International Shipping

7-14 business days depending on destination. Customs duties and taxes may apply.

Insurance & Security

Your jewelry's safety is our top priority:

  • All shipments are fully insured against loss, theft, or damage during transit
  • Packages are discreetly packed without branding to ensure security
  • Tamper-evident packaging ensures your order arrives intact
  • Signature confirmation is required for all deliveries
  • High-value orders may require additional verification at delivery

Shipping Charges

  • Free Shipping: Available on all orders above ₹10,000 within India
  • Standard Shipping: ₹150 for orders below ₹10,000
  • Express Shipping: ₹350 (calculated at checkout based on location)
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Additional ₹100 handling fee applies
  • International: Calculated based on destination and order value

Delivery Areas

We deliver to most locations across India including:

  • All major metropolitan cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Pune)
  • Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities through our courier partners
  • Remote areas may require additional delivery time
  • International shipping available to select countries (contact us for details)

Important Notes

  • Delivery times are estimates and may vary due to unforeseen circumstances
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by courier services or natural disasters
  • Please ensure the shipping address is accurate and complete
  • Failed delivery attempts may result in additional charges for re-delivery
  • For high-value orders, we may contact you to verify delivery details
