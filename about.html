Our Story

Sai Kiran Jewelleries was founded in 2002 with a vision to provide authentic, high-quality gold jewelry to the people of Vijayawada. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted jewelry showrooms in the region.

Our A/C showroom in Patamata, Vijayawada showcases an extensive collection of traditional and contemporary designs. From intricate bridal sets to modern daily wear, we have something for every occasion.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers through transparency, quality, and exceptional service. Every piece that leaves our showroom carries our commitment to excellence.
